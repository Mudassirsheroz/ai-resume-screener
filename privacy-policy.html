🧠 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2025

Welcome to CV Screener Pro. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use CV Screener Pro, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account Info: Your name and email address (via Google OAuth login)
  • Uploaded Files: CV/resume files you upload for analysis (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  • Job Descriptions: Text you enter or save within the app
  • Analysis Results: AI-generated screening results stored in your account

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity via Google Login
  • To analyze CVs against job descriptions using AI
  • To save your screening history and job descriptions for your personal use
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Each user can only access their own data — we enforce strict Row Level Security policies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for secure login
  • Groq AI (Llama 3) — for CV analysis
  • Supabase — for data storage
  • Streamlit — for app hosting

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties except as required to operate the app (e.g., sending CV text to Groq AI for analysis).

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your saved job descriptions at any time from within the app. To request full data deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

We take data security seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely. Access is restricted to authenticated users only.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of using the service

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
